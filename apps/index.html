Headlines - (news)

Headlines is a remarkably simple news app. As the name implies, today's latest headlines can finally be browsed quickly and easily- there are no long articles to traverse, unlike other apps. If the user finds an article particularly interesting, tapping on it will reveal more information, including the article's introduction, and a button to read the article in its entirety.

Headlines adapts to you and your interests. Choose from a list of numerous categories and over 32 world-renowned sources covering politics, sports, business, and much more. Preferances are saved in the background, so feel free to change them at anytime.

Proximity - (map)

Proximity is a map like no other. It uses iPhone's GPS, gyroscrope, and a connection to Google's Places API↗ to locate and identify locations within a certain radius of the user's current position. Then, when you select one of these locations, it's relative direction and distance are projected over the device's live camera feed, effectively tracking the location in real time.

Bounce - (game)

A simple arcade game for casual players of all ages. The game's objective is to protect the black square at all times. Strategicaly orrient the square in order to ricoshet the blue ball into the red, pushing it away from the square. If the red ball ever touches the square, one of the three lives is lost. How long will you last?
